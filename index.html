L A N Y

A l b u m s :

Make out (2015)

Make Out is the first EP by dream-pop band LANY. It was released on December 11, 2015. At this point in time, LANY embarked on American tours supporting Twin Shadow, Tove Styrke, X Ambassadors, Troye Sivan and Halsey.

LANY (2017)

LANY is LANY’s debut album. It was released on June 30, 2017. Following a music video release for “ILYSB,” the album announcement came on February 25 in a short Twitter video. In this video, “Good Girls” was announced as the second single and released shortly after on March 3. “It Was Love”, “The Breakup” and “13” were also mentioned in the video, to be released as singles before the album.

Gg bb xx (2021)

Gg bb xx is the fourth studio album by LANY. Released on September 11, 2021 through Side Street Entertainment and Polydor Records, it serves as a follow-up to their third studio album, Mama's Boy (2020). It is the band's final album to feature keyboardist and guitarist Leslie Priest, who left the band the following year.

Malibu Nights (2018)

“Malibu Nights” serves as the title track for Malibu Nights, as well as the closure. The song is about someone’s thoughts after a breakup. “Malibu Nights” was teased through the release of the album trailer published on 6 September 2018.

Mama's Boy (2020)

Mama’s boy is the third studio album by LANY. The project was announced on their social media on April 30th, 2020—explaining that they would release it later this year. The message was accompanied by an album trailer. Frontman Paul Klein revealed part of the tracklist for the album on March 23rd, 2020.

LANY are an American pop rock band from Los Angeles. Formed in Nashville in 2014, the band consists of Paul Jason Klein and Jake Clifford Goss. Their songs are in the genres of indie pop, alternative rock, pop rock, synth-pop, pop-punk, dream pop, and pop. They are signed to Polydor and Interscope Records.
